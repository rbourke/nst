How does Mosquito Drops work?

  • Mosquito Drops is a silicone-based liquid which self-spreads across the surface of standing water, forming a very thin film.
  • Silicones have a low surface tension and, when the film is present, the immature mosquito stages cannot attach themselves to the surface to breathe – causing them to drown.
  • Applied from the bottle or drum, and it will self-spread quickly and completely – even if there is a large amount of vegetation on the surface- The silicone film will remain effective for 30 days.

How does WaterGuard work?

  • WaterGuard is a unique liquid which spreads over the surface of water to form a very thin film and reduce EVAPORATION.
  • Water losses from evaporation are huge: a 10 hectare dam (25 acres) can lose up to 800,000 litres each day in summer, or more than 5 million litres per week. * Trials with WaterGuard have achieved evaporation savings of at least 50%
